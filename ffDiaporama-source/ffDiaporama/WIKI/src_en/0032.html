Generally speaking the main differences between these devices are:
  • Screen size and maximal resolution
  • Operating system on the device
Consult the documentation for your equipment or search the Internet to determine these two characteristics.

As far as the resolution is concerned, note that most devices can play videos designed for lower resolution devices without difficulty.
